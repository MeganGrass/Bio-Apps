
--------------
Bio Zero
MHP -> JPG

MeganGrass
The-Horror.com
--------------

Convert files with MHP extension to JPG format.

This app has not been tested with files from the Bio Zero Trial Edition.


---------
USAGE #01
---------

Commandline is supported, for multiple MHP conversion at once.
Example:

MHP.EXE blood.mhp
MHP.EXE type09.mhp
MHP.EXE omk002.mhp


---------
USAGE #02
---------

Simply "Drag & Drop" a MHP file onto the executable. With this method,
only one (1) MHP can be handled at once.
